Upload CloudTrucks Looker Export

Export the Fleet First - Truck Lease Payment by Day report from Looker, then upload it here. The app will split each payment into principal & interest, and generate a QBO-ready journal entry CSV for you to download.

Expected columns: Payment Date, Team Name, Vendor Program, Subheader (VIN), Team ID, Payment Amount
How it works
  1. Each FLEET_FIRST_TRUCK_LEASE row → 3-line JE:
    Dr 1800 Due from CloudTrucks / Cr 1951 Lease Receivable (principal) / Cr 4300 Interest Income
  2. Each FLEET_FIRST_MAINTENANCE row → 2-line JE:
    Dr 1800 Due from CloudTrucks / Cr 2603 Maintenance Deposit
  3. Principal/interest split uses the weekly pro-rata from each truck's amortization schedule.
  4. Duplicate payments (same truck, date, amount) are skipped automatically.
  5. Download the output CSV and import via QBO → Settings → Import Data → Journal Entries.
